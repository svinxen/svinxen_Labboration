Bonnie

Bonnie was an impuls, just like my own dog Atlas, but for my best friend Elina. With that said, it was the best impuls buy she ever made. Bonnie is a true Husky, very energetic, talkative and all over the place.

When she first got to her forever-home she was a very scared and nervous little pup, even scared around little Atlas. Now Bonnie is magestic, confident and even though not the oldest, the leader of the pack.
